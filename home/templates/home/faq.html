FAQ

Ordering and delivery
  • How long is the delivery time?
    • 2–5 working days within Sweden. EU 3–7 working days. You will receive tracking by e-mail when the package leaves our warehouse.
  • How much does shipping cost?
    • We show shipping costs at checkout. Free shipping over 500 SEK within Sweden.
Returns and complaints
  • Can I make a return?
    • Yes, 30 days open purchase on unused item in original condition. Please contact us before returning.
  • The item was damaged – what do I do?
    • Email the order number + pictures of the damage and we will arrange a replacement/new product.
Payment
  • What payment methods do you accept?
    • Card (Visa/Mastercard), Klarna, and other methods via Stripe.
  • Currency and prices
    • Prices are displayed in SEK and paid in SEK. Your bank may display the equivalent amount in your local currency.
Products & stock
  • An item is out of stock - will it come back in?
    • Register your interest via the product page and we will notify you when it is back.
  • Can you package as a gift?
    • Yes, select gift wrapping at checkout (if available) or leave a comment.
Account & orders
  • Where can I find my previous orders?
    • Log in → My Account → Orders.
  • Can I update the address after ordering?
    • Please contact us immediately – we will try to help as long as the order has not been shipped.
